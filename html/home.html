WHO ARE WE?---------

ZINGLEES partners with parents and schools all over India to facilitate their understanding of how children learn across high schools in India. We act as an independent evaluation partner, focused primarily upon providing diagnostic reports of every child to be able to give each child the unparalleled attention that they deserve.
Schools
Examinations have become mere formalities. They have become rituals without a purpose. The question papers are set without a purpose, just following the template to test the memory and award marks. With the voice for learning outcome gaining strength, it is high time schools have to move from irrelevant examinations to much needed Evaluations.
Parents
Parents pin their hopes on schools for around 15 years and are in for a surprise shock at the end of school education or competitive entrance examinations. Parents do not have a platform to diagnose the child's standing in various subjects at regular intervals and also do not have any dependable analytics to closely monitor the progress and identify the problem areas of the child.

---------KEY OFFERINGS---------
Evaluation Partner

Partner with us and allow us to take over your complete evaluation process. We cover daily tests, practice tests, weekly and monthly tests, term tests, revision assessments and final year exams. We also provide detailed analytics on each child in order for schools and teachers to accurately monitor their advancement and level of proficiency over a defined period of time.

National Competitive Examination Practice Centre (NCEPC)

It’s just not perseverance and hard work that’ll help a student clear a national level competitive examination. Practice is the key. A practice that provides you detailed feedbacks highlighting the strengths, needs weakness of every student will help them hone their skills accordingly and will be more confident and equipped to face the competitive test irrespective of whether they are conducted at a national or international level.

Learning Achievement Plans

In this proposed vertical, we will support the schools with the list of outcomes, plans and activities to achieve them, a platform to exhibit the outcome. Will provide all the content, training, and evaluations that are critical parts in the new pedagogy. Supporting the schools in achieving their new goals, without leaning heavily on teachers and other limitations will be welcomed by schools.

Technology Support Services

We've reached a stage where technology has a critical role to play in any domain including schools. While there are too many vendors offering different technology based solutions to schools, only affordable schools are able to subscribe to those services and a majority is deprived of these good to haves as they cannot afford to these paid services.
