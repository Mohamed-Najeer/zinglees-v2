SCHOOLS---------

Majority of the schools have challenges in achieving their purpose of their founding or existence.year after report gives us the learning that we do expected learning in schools. There could be varied reasons for the schools to quote for missing out on achieving the intended class/age appropriate learning outcomes. The key point.

Learning outcomes are specific to individuals. We will not be able achieve learning without understanding the understanding of every child in every concept in every subject. In addition, schols/teachers should have a clear idea on the strength, weaknesses and specific need of every child to support them in achieving the learning.

We are zinglees through out mindful and purpose driven evaluation systems, Will be able to squarely meet the requirements of the schools and teachers and provide them with detailed diagnostic reports of every child.

We partner with schools as evaluation partners and taken charge of complete evaluations covering daily practice tests, weekly tests, monthly tests, term tests, division tests, and the final year exams.

As a part out offering. We also support schools with detailed analytics of every child on their progress and proficiency level progression over a selected period.
